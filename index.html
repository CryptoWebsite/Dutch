How to Buy

To purchase Dutch Coin, you can use a DEX like Uniswap. Make sure to connect your wallet and swap ETH for DUTCH.

  1. Open Uniswap and connect your wallet.
  2. Search for DUTCH and set the amount to swap.
  3. Confirm the transaction, and you're set!
